# Operational-risk -- Consumption Contracts

This directory lists all `operational-risk` contracts in the io-reporting data product.

- [Data Freshness](/operational-risk/op_risk/data_freshness.md) -- Per-canonical-domain data freshness watermarks with query-time age and status classification (fresh/warning/critical/no_data).
- [Risk Item](/operational-risk/op_risk/risk_item.md) -- Operational risk items — live UNION of domain arms (production, warehouse, quality, logistics, data_trust) with query-time effective_severity and time_horizon.
